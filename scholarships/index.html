Opportunity Scholarships


We deeply believe in creating an inclusive and diverse conference and want to make sure that everyone is able to participate.

We are accepting applications from diverse groups to attend for free, and for some, with hotel and/or airfare included. Application deadline is September 30th, 2019.

We also want to make sure that local students and others residents are able to attend, so the Hawaiʻian community is encouraged to apply!
